# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Online shoppers looking for objective quality and price analysis of clothing. They are often uncertain about material quality, fabric blends, and whether they are paying for a label versus actual durability.

## Product Purpose
To provide objective, transparent scoring for products across the web, giving shoppers confidence before they buy. It acts as a trusted advisor right in the browser, showing if a product is "Worth it", "Fair", or a "Skip", and surfacing better-value alternatives.

## Brand Personality
Transparent, Objective, Premium

## Anti-references
- SaaS clichés (e.g., big number, small label, gradient accents)
- Em dashes (use commas/colons/periods instead)
- Cheap or overly "bot-like" UI (must feel like a trustworthy paid product)

## Design Principles
- **Rules over AI:** Scoring must be deterministic and fully auditable by the user. AI is only used to summarize reviews.
- **Show, Don't Tell:** Use the UI (badges, scores, mono fonts) to convey the rigorous data behind the verdict.
- **Accessibility as a Feature:** Verdicts must convey meaning by text+icon, not color alone. Strict contrast and screen reader support.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Strict Accessibility (High contrast, strict screen reader support)
